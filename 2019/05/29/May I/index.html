May I

May I stand unshaken
在世界的崩溃中
Amid,amidst a crash of worlds?
我能毫不动摇吗？

Did I hear a thunder?
我听到的是雷声
Did I hear you break?
还是你破碎的声音？
I can’t quite remember
是什么将我引至此路
Just what guided me this way
我早已记不清

The pines,they often whisper
松树 它们常低语
They whisper what no tongue can tell
却没有人能够听清
He who drinks from the deep water
那些啜饮于深渚的人
May he konw the depths of the well
愿他们知晓井下的深渊

Mmm,oh,traveler
旅人
What have you seen?
你看到了什么？
Were there crossroads
你去过的那些十字路口
Where you been,Where you been?
它们现在引你向何方？
I once was standing tall
我曾昂着头不愿低垂
Now I feel my back is against the wall
而今已背抵高墙 无路可退

May I stand unshaken?
我可以毫不动摇地站在世界的崩溃中吗？
